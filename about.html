About NayePankh Foundation

NayePankh Foundation was initiated during the COVID-19 pandemic to help people in difficult times. Today it works in education, food distribution, healthcare, menstrual hygiene awareness, and clothing drives.

Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to basic needs and opportunities.
